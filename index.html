About Us

Admissions Consulting focuses on providing a diverse range of medical school admission consulting services to pre-med students applying to Canadian medical schools. Our dedicated professionals will assist students starting from medical school selection, application process, personalized essays, Casper test preparations, to providing interview tips. We provide personalized services to cater to your specific strengths and needs. MN Admissions Consulting strives to showcase your strengths to medical students and help you achieve your career goals

Pre-Med Mentoring

×

Through extensive research and past experiences, MN Admissions Consulting has identified an unmet student need. Many pre-medical students seek admission consulting immediately prior to application or during the current application cycle. However, in our experience students that start seeking help earlier tend to have better outcomes. Therefore, MN Admission Consulting has introduced a new service to provide students the opportunity to begin their medical school admission journey earlier and have better outcomes. This service is geared towards providing students with the mentorship needed to build a strong foundation that will pave an easier path towards their medical school journey. This service is geared towards undergraduate/graduate students that are not applying in this admission cycle but would like to get tips on improving their overall application for future cycles. This is a longitudinal service that will pair you with an experienced medical student that can help you build a CV that will land you the medical school of your choice. Please email us to hear more about this option.
